Frequently Asked Questions

How does ChartAlert work? +

You get a unique webhook URL from your dashboard. Add this URL to any TradingView alert. When the alert fires, we capture a live chart screenshot and post it to your Discord channel — fully automated.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes! You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your dashboard. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept Credit Cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal through our secure payment partner LemonSqueezy.

What happens when I hit my daily limit? +

Alerts beyond your daily limit are dropped and won't be forwarded to Discord. Your counter resets at midnight UTC each day. Upgrade to Pro for 500 alerts/day.
